About Us

LiveFit is a smart nutrition and fitness platform created to help people understand their daily food intake and make healthier lifestyle choices.

Our goal is to simplify nutrition tracking by presenting data in a clear, visual, and user- friendly way. LiveFit allows users to monitor essential nutrients, receive daily health tips, and stay motivated throughout their fitness journey.

Designed as a complete solution, LiveFit supports everyone—from beginners to fitness- conscious individuals—by promoting balance, consistency, and long-term wellness.
